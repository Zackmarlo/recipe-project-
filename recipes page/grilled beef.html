Grilled beef recipe



ingredients

  • 1/2 cup aged balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 6 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onion
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1/4 cup fresh rosemary leaves
  • 2 pounds beef top sirloin
  • 4 skewers

recipe

  1. Whisk balsamic vinegar, olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, green onion, salt, black pepper, cayenne, and honey together in a bowl.
  2. Add rosemary and set aside.
  3. Cut beef into cubes about 1 1/4- to 1 1/2- inch square and add to marinade.
  4. Wrap and refrigerate overnight, tossing occasionally if possible.
  5. Thread beef on 4 skewers, and pat dry with paper towels before grilling.
  6. Grill over high heat, basting occasionally with marinade, about 5 minutes per side for medium, or until beef reaches desired doneness.
  7. Remove meat from grill and let rest 5 minutes before serving.
  8. Meanwhile, bring remaining marinade to a boil in a saucepan.
  9. Strain and use as a sauce for serving.
